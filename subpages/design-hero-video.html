Design Hero video

Spring 2024

motion design

how do you bridge graphic design and interactive creative code to give the user control?

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Blender
  • Sound Design
  • Music Composition

project brief: create a 1-minute long animated video honoring the work of a chosen “design hero”; someone you admire whose design work inspires you

for this project i: created a video outlining the philosophies of Kiel Mutschelknaus using his work as stylistic reference, as well as arranging a soundtrack to accompany it
